Gulab Jamun

Ingredients:

  • Bread
  • Sugar
  • Oil

Recipe:

  1. Mix milk powder, all-purpose flour, ghee, and a pinch of baking soda in a bowl.
  2. Gradually add milk and knead to form a smooth, soft dough
  3. Divide the dough into small portions, shape them into balls, and deep fry until golden brown.
